Dynamis

Rewards from Dynamis comprise of relic armor and three types of ancient beastmen currency that is used to upgrade relic weapons. The right to lot these items can be earned through attendance and participation.
Select $ to lot ancient currency or select two AF for a city. Currently, only one AF may be selected for northlands. When editing the database, check the checkbox below each area if you have not completed that area yet and uncheck it later when you get the 'win'.
